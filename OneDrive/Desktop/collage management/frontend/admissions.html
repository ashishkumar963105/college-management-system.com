Admission Process

1

Online Application

Fill out the online application form with required details

2

Entrance Exam

Appear for entrance examination or submit valid scores

3

Document Verification

Submit all required documents for verification

4

Counseling

Attend counseling session and select your program

5

Fee Payment

Complete fee payment to confirm admission

Important Dates

  • Application Start: March 1, 2026
  • Application End: May 31, 2026
  • Entrance Exam: June 15, 2026
  • Results: June 30, 2026
  • Counseling: July 5-15, 2026

Required Documents

  • 10th & 12th Mark Sheets
  • Transfer Certificate
  • Entrance Exam Score Card
  • Passport Size Photos
  • ID Proof (Aadhar/PAN)

Eligibility Criteria

  • Minimum 60% in 12th
  • Physics, Chemistry, Maths mandatory
  • Valid entrance exam score
  • Age: 17-25 years
  • Medical fitness certificate
